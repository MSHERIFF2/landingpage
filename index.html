About the project AgriSmart

What bring about the project AgriSmart is as a result of the issues farmers face in majority of Nigeria Community in terms of what price are they going to sell there farm produces. This can be achieve if they know the current price of farm product in the market. Secondly weather condition is a major factor that determin what to farm which majority of farmers find it difficult to differentiate the different weather condition and also farmers lack knowledge of modern farming tips. therefore this project solve all the stated problems and makes farmers to farm smart hence AgriSmart.
